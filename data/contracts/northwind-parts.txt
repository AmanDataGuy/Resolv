SUPPLY AGREEMENT — NORTHWIND PARTS CO
Master Services Agreement, Section 3: Delivery Obligations

Clause 3.1 — Notification of Delay
Supplier shall notify Buyer within twenty-four (24) hours of becoming aware of
any circumstance likely to delay delivery beyond the agreed delivery window.

Master Services Agreement, Section 4: Late Delivery and Penalties

Clause 4.1 — Late Delivery Penalty
Where Supplier fails to deliver any order by the estimated delivery date, Buyer
shall be entitled to a service credit equal to one and one-half percent (1.5%)
of the shipment value for each order affected, calculated against the total
invoiced value of the delayed shipment.

Clause 4.2 — Repeated Delay
Three or more late deliveries within a rolling sixty (60) day period entitle
Buyer to require a corrective action plan or to source the affected volume from
an alternate supplier without penalty to Buyer.

Master Services Agreement, Section 5: Product Quality and Rejection

Clause 5.1 — Inspection and Rejection
Buyer may inspect any shipment within seventy-two (72) hours of receipt and
reject any unit that fails to conform to the agreed specification. Supplier
shall issue a full credit note within ten (10) business days of collection of
rejected goods.

Master Services Agreement, Section 6: Supplier Capacity

Clause 6.1 — Capacity Shortfall Notice
Where Supplier cannot fulfill a confirmed order due to a capacity shortfall,
Supplier must notify Buyer within twelve (12) hours of identifying the
shortfall.

Master Services Agreement, Section 7: Customs and Cross-Border Shipments

Clause 7.1 — Documentation Responsibility
For any shipment crossing an international border, Supplier is responsible for
providing complete and accurate customs documentation at least forty-eight (48)
hours prior to the scheduled ship date.

Master Services Agreement, Section 8: Invoicing and Payment Disputes

Clause 8.1 — Invoice Accuracy
Supplier shall issue invoices matching the corresponding Purchase Order in unit
price, quantity, and applicable discounts. Buyer may raise a price dispute in
writing within seventy-two (72) hours of receiving a non-matching invoice.
